# Call Flow

This document describes the planned receptionist workflow.

## Basic Flow

1. Customer sends a message or starts a call.
2. The assistant collects name, reason, contact details, and preferred time.
3. The assistant answers simple FAQs from approved business information.
4. Complex requests are marked for human review.
5. Appointment or callback requests are summarized.
6. The business owner receives a daily summary.

## Design Principles

- keep responses short and helpful
- collect only necessary information
- never confirm unavailable appointments without integration
- escalate uncertain requests to a human
- provide clear summaries
